Week 3: Ledgers Recording & Malaysia SST Tax Tariffs 第 3 周：分类账记录与马来西亚 SST 销售与服务税

Step-by-Step Guide 分步指南

1. Transaction Ledgers

Routine entries are recorded into Sales journals (credit sales to debtors), Purchase journals (credit purchases from suppliers), and cash books. Double entry rule: Debit Assets/Expenses increase; Credit Liabilities/Revenue increase.

2. Malaysia SST & Tax Tariff System

Sales and Service Tax (SST) applies standard tariffs: **6% or 8% Service Tax** (e.g. hospitality, digital, consult services) and **5% or 10% Sales Tax** (imported/manufactured goods). SQL/Million use tax codes: **SR** (Service tax standard rate), **TX** (Purchase input tax), **OS** (Out of scope).

1. 交易明细账录入

日常交易录入销售日记账（债务人信贷销售）、采购日记账（供应商信贷采购）和现金簿。双向复式记账规则：借方 (Debit) 资产/费用增加；贷方 (Credit) 负债/收入增加。

2. 马来西亚 SST 销售与服务税制度

销售与服务税 (SST) 采用标准税率：**6% 或 8% 的服务税**（例如餐饮、数字、咨询服务）以及 **5% 或 10% 的销售税**（进口/制造商品）。SQL/Million 中常用税码：**SR**（服务税标准税率）、**TX**（采购进项税额）、**OS**（超出范围）。
