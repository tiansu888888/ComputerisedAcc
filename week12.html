Week 12: AI Analytics & Financial Forecasting 第 12 周：AI 辅助财务分析与资金预测

Step-by-Step Guide 分步指南

1. Financial Dashboards

Dashboards visualize key financial metrics in real-time, tracking ratios: Current Ratio (Assets/Liabilities), Debtor Days (collection periods), and Net Profit Margins.

2. Cash Flow Forecasting

By analyzing past ledger transaction patterns, AI engines generate cash flow projections. This helps managers anticipate cash deficits and plan working capital reserves.

1. 财务数据看板

数据看板实时可视化关键财务指标，追踪各种比率：流动比率（资产/负债）、应收账款周转天数（催款周期）和净利润率。

2. 现金流滚动预测

通过分析过去的分类账交易模式，AI 引擎生成现金流预测预测。这有助于管理层提前预测资金缺口并规划营运资金储备。

🤖 AI Integration Playbook 🤖 AI 实践指南

AI Prompting for Ratios:

Prompt: 'Here is a Trial Balance: cash = RM 50k, debtors = RM 30k, creditors = RM 20k. Write the formula to calculate the Quick Ratio, and interpret what it means for liquidity.'
Result: AI states Quick Ratio is 4.0, representing excellent ability to pay debts without selling stocks.

AI 辅助步骤：

Prompt 提示词： “这里有一份试算平衡表：现金 = RM 50k，债务人 = RM 30k，债权人 = RM 20k。请写出计算速动比率的公式，并解释其对流动性的意义。”
效果： AI 指出速动比率为 4.0，表示在不售卖存货的情况下具有极佳的偿债能力。

Tips 提示

Dashboards are only as good as the underlying data. Ensure all daily purchase and sales invoices are posted before running predictive cash flow models.
数据看板的好坏完全取决于底层数据是否准确。在运行预测性现金流模型之前，确保已录入所有日常采购和销售发票。
