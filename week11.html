Step-by-Step Guide 分步指南

1. Integrated Ecosystems

Modern accounting links directly to third-party business apps: e-commerce (Shopify), inventory platforms, and payment gateways. Direct integration eliminates manual journal voucher duplication.

2. Sales & Payment Gateway Sync

Processing online transactions automatically triggers two ledger postings: debiting Stripe/PayPal clearing account (sales receipts) and crediting Sales Revenue + SST payable accounts.

1. 数字化集成生态系统

现代会计直接连接第三方业务应用：电子商务 (Shopify)、库存管理平台和支付网关。直接集成消除了人工重复录入日记账凭单的工作。

2. 销售与支付网关同步

处理在线交易时自动触发两笔分类账过账：借记 Stripe/PayPal 清算账户（销售收款），贷记销售收入和应付 SST 账户。

🤖 AI Integration Playbook 🤖 AI 实践指南

AI Prompting for Integration Rules:

Prompt: 'Our Stripe payments deduct a 2% fee before bank transfer. Write the double-entry mapping formula to record Shopify Sales (RM 100), Stripe Fee (2%), and Bank Deposit.'
Result: AI delivers mappings: Debit Bank 98, Debit Stripe Fee 2, Credit Sales Revenue 100.

AI 辅助步骤：

Prompt 提示词： “我们的 Stripe 支付在银行转账前扣除 2% 的手续费。请写出复式记账映射公式来记录 Shopify 销售 (RM 100)、Stripe 手续费 (2%) 和银行存款。”
效果： AI 给出分录：借记银行 98，借记 Stripe 手续费 2，贷记销售收入 100。

Tips 提示

Always map payment gateway processing fees to a dedicated expense ledger account. Do not net off sales revenues, as tax authorities require auditing gross sales amounts.
务必将支付网关的手续费映射到专用的费用科目。切勿直接扣减销售收入，因为税务机关审计时要求查看总销售金额。
