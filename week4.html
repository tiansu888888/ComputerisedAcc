Week 4: General Journal & Payroll Transactions 第 4 周：日记分录账与薪资业务处理

Step-by-Step Guide 分步指南

1. General Journal Adjustments

General Journals are used for non-routine entries like opening balances, depreciation, and year-end accruals. Double entry balances are strictly checked: total debits must equal total credits before saving.

2. Salary Accruals & EPF/SOCSO Taxes

Monthly salary journals debit Wages/Salaries Expense, EPF Employer, and SOCSO Employer, while crediting Accrued Salaries, Employee EPF/SOCSO deductions, and PCB (Monthly Tax Deduction) payables.

1. 普通日记账调整

普通日记账用于非日常分录，如期初余额、折旧和年末应计项目。系统严格检查借贷平衡：保存前总借项必须等于总贷项。

2. 薪资计提与 EPF/SOCSO 马来西亚税费

每月薪资日记账借记工资/薪金费用、EPF 雇主部分和 SOCSO 雇主部分，同时贷记应付薪资、员工 EPF/SOCSO 扣款以及 PCB（每月预扣税款）应付款项。
